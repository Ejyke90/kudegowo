## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Logo system must communicate secure educational payments
The system SHALL provide a comprehensive logo system that visually communicates security, education, and payment functionality to users.

#### Scenario: User perceives security from logo
- **WHEN** a parent views the KudiKlass logo for the first time
- **THEN** they SHALL immediately understand this is a secure payment platform
- **AND** they SHALL recognize educational context
- **AND** they SHALL feel confident making financial transactions

#### Scenario: Logo conveys trustworthiness
- **WHEN** school administrators evaluate the platform
- **THEN** the logo SHALL communicate professionalism and reliability
- **AND** they SHALL feel confident recommending to parents

### Requirement: Logo must work across all digital platforms
The logo system SHALL maintain visual clarity and impact across web, mobile, and social media platforms.

#### Scenario: Logo displays on mobile devices
- **WHEN** users access KudiKlass on smartphones
- **THEN** the logo SHALL be clearly visible and recognizable
- **AND** it SHALL load quickly without quality loss

#### Scenario: Logo appears in social media profiles
- **WHEN** KudiKlass logo is used as social media avatar
- **THEN** it SHALL be recognizable at small sizes
- **AND** it SHALL maintain brand consistency across platforms

### Requirement: Logo must include variations for different contexts
The system SHALL provide multiple logo variations optimized for different use cases and sizes.

#### Scenario: Primary logo for main branding
- **WHEN** displaying the logo on website headers
- **THEN** the primary logo SHALL include full brand elements
- **AND** it SHALL be optimized for larger display areas

#### Scenario: Simplified logo for small spaces
- **WHEN** displaying logo as favicon or app icon
- **THEN** a simplified version SHALL be available
- **AND** it SHALL maintain brand recognition at minimal sizes

#### Scenario: Monochrome logo for documents
- **WHEN** printing logo on receipts or invoices
- **THEN** a monochrome version SHALL be available
- **AND** it SHALL remain clear and professional

### Requirement: Logo must incorporate security visual elements
The logo design SHALL include visual elements that communicate security and trust.

#### Scenario: Security iconography recognition
- **WHEN** users view the logo
- **THEN** they SHALL identify security-related symbols (shield, lock, etc.)
- **AND** these elements SHALL be integrated naturally into the design

#### Scenario: Trust signal communication
- **WHEN** parents consider making payments
- **THEN** the logo SHALL serve as a visual trust signal
- **AND** it SHALL reduce anxiety about financial transactions

### Requirement: Logo must reflect educational context
The logo system SHALL incorporate elements that clearly indicate educational focus.

#### Scenario: Educational element identification
- **WHEN** users view the logo
- **THEN** they SHALL recognize educational symbols (books, graduation cap, etc.)
- **AND** these elements SHALL distinguish it from generic payment platforms

#### Scenario: Target audience relevance
- **WHEN** Nigerian parents see the logo
- **THEN** they SHALL understand this serves educational needs
- **AND** they SHALL feel the platform is specifically designed for schools

### Requirement: Logo must support positive ethical user experience
The logo design SHALL promote feelings of safety, reliability, and positive user experience.

#### Scenario: Emotional response to logo
- **WHEN** users interact with the brand
- **THEN** the logo SHALL evoke feelings of security and confidence
- **AND** it SHALL create positive associations with educational payments

#### Scenario: Accessibility and inclusivity
- **WHEN** users with visual impairments encounter the logo
- **THEN** it SHALL meet accessibility standards
- **AND** it SHALL be distinguishable for users with color vision deficiencies
