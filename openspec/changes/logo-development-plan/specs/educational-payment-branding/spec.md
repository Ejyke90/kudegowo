## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Logo must clearly indicate educational focus
The logo system SHALL communicate that the platform is specifically designed for educational payments.

#### Scenario: Educational context recognition
- **WHEN** users view the logo for the first time
- **THEN** they SHALL immediately understand the educational context
- **AND** they SHALL recognize this is not a generic payment platform

#### Scenario: School payment specialization
- **WHEN** school administrators evaluate the platform
- **THEN** the logo SHALL communicate specialization in school payments
- **AND** it SHALL differentiate from general financial services

### Requirement: Logo must incorporate educational visual elements
The logo design SHALL include visual elements that clearly reference education and learning.

#### Scenario: Educational symbol integration
- **WHEN** users examine the logo design
- **THEN** educational symbols (books, graduation caps, etc.) SHALL be visible
- **AND** these elements SHALL be naturally integrated into the design

#### Scenario: Learning environment representation
- **WHEN** parents view the logo
- **THEN** it SHALL evoke positive associations with education
- **AND** it SHALL represent a supportive learning environment

### Requirement: Logo must reflect payment functionality
The logo system SHALL clearly indicate payment and financial transaction capabilities.

#### Scenario: Payment functionality recognition
- **WHEN** users view the logo
- **THEN** payment symbols SHALL be recognizable
- **AND** users SHALL understand this is a payment platform

#### Scenario: Financial transaction clarity
- **WHEN** users consider making payments
- **THEN** the logo SHALL reinforce payment capabilities
- **AND** it SHALL communicate ease of financial transactions

### Requirement: Logo must appeal to Nigerian educational context
The logo design SHALL be culturally appropriate and appealing to the Nigerian educational market.

#### Scenario: Cultural relevance
- **WHEN** Nigerian users view the logo
- **THEN** it SHALL feel culturally appropriate
- **AND** it SHALL resonate with local educational values

#### Scenario: Local market connection
- **WHEN** school communities see the logo
- **THEN** it SHALL feel familiar and trustworthy
- **AND** it SHALL reflect understanding of local needs

### Requirement: Logo must support parent-school relationship
The logo system SHALL communicate the platform's role in facilitating parent-school financial relationships.

#### Scenario: Parent-school partnership
- **WHEN** parents view the logo
- **THEN** it SHALL suggest partnership with schools
- **AND** it SHALL communicate collaboration in education

#### Scenario: Trust in educational ecosystem
- **WHEN** school administrators see the logo
- **THEN** it SHALL represent a trusted educational partner
- **AND** it SHALL enhance school-parent relationships

### Requirement: Logo must convey positive educational outcomes
The logo design SHALL associate the platform with positive educational achievements and student success.

#### Scenario: Educational success association
- **WHEN** users interact with the brand
- **THEN** the logo SHALL evoke positive educational outcomes
- **AND** it SHALL suggest investment in student success

#### Scenario: Future-oriented messaging
- **WHEN** parents make educational payments
- **THEN** the logo SHALL reinforce positive future impact
- **AND** it SHALL communicate investment in education
