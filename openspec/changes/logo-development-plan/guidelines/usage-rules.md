# Logo Usage Rules and Restrictions

## Overview

This document outlines the specific rules, restrictions, and best practices for using the KudiKlass logo system. Adherence to these guidelines ensures brand consistency, legal compliance, and optimal brand representation across all applications.

## Logo Usage Rules

### General Usage Principles

#### Core Principles
1. **Consistency**: Use logos consistently across all applications
2. **Clarity**: Ensure logos are always clear and recognizable
3. **Respect**: Treat the logo with respect and professionalism
4. **Integrity**: Maintain the logo's visual integrity at all times
5. **Quality**: Use only high-quality, approved logo files

#### Brand Representation
- The logo represents the entire KudiKlass brand
- Logo usage reflects on brand quality and professionalism
- Proper usage builds brand recognition and trust
- Inconsistent usage damages brand integrity

### Logo Variations Usage

#### Primary Logo
**When to Use**:
- Website headers and main navigation
- Marketing materials and brochures
- Business cards and stationery
- Official documents and contracts
- High-impact brand applications

**Requirements**:
- Minimum size: 120px × 120px
- Clear space: 0.5x logo height minimum
- Background: Clean, uncluttered
- Color: Full brand colors or approved variations

#### Simplified Logo
**When to Use**:
- Mobile applications and responsive design
- Small digital applications
- Email signatures
- Social media profile pictures
- Space-constrained applications

**Requirements**:
- Minimum size: 64px × 64px
- Clear space: 0.25x logo height minimum
- Background: Clean, high contrast
- Color: Full colors or simplified palette

#### Icon Logo
**When to Use**:
- Favicon and browser icons
- App icons and shortcuts
- UI elements and buttons
- Small decorative elements
- Navigation indicators

**Requirements**:
- Minimum size: 32px × 32px
- Clear space: 2px minimum
- Background: High contrast
- Color: Single color or simplified palette

#### Monochrome Logo
**When to Use**:
- Single-color printing
- Documents and forms
- Legal and official documents
- Fax applications
- Engraving or embossing

**Requirements**:
- Minimum size: 64px × 64px
- Clear space: 0.25x logo height minimum
- Color: Single color (typically dark blue or black)
- Background: High contrast

## Logo Restrictions

### Prohibited Modifications

#### Visual Modifications
❌ **Never alter the logo's proportions**
- Do not stretch or distort the logo
- Do not change the aspect ratio
- Do not crop or trim the logo
- Do not rotate the logo (except for specific design purposes)

❌ **Never change logo colors**
- Do not use unauthorized colors
- Do not apply gradients or effects
- Do not change color relationships
- Do not use brand colors incorrectly

❌ **Never add or remove elements**
- Do not add text or graphics to the logo
- Do not remove logo elements
- Do not combine with other logos
- Do not create hybrid logos

❌ **Never apply effects or filters**
- Do not apply drop shadows (except specified)
- Do not apply blur or distortion effects
- Do not apply transparency effects
- Do not apply artistic filters

#### Context Restrictions
❌ **Never use on inappropriate backgrounds**
- Do not place on busy or patterned backgrounds
- Do not place on low-contrast backgrounds
- Do not place on competing brand backgrounds
- Do not place on inappropriate content

❌ **Never use in inappropriate contexts**
- Do not associate with controversial content
- Do not use in political contexts
- Do not use with competing products
- Do not use in misleading ways

### Size Restrictions

#### Minimum Size Requirements
- **Primary Logo**: 120px × 120px absolute minimum
- **Simplified Logo**: 64px × 64px absolute minimum
- **Icon Logo**: 32px × 32px absolute minimum
- **Monochrome Logo**: 64px × 64px absolute minimum

#### Size Recommendations
- **Large Format**: 240px × 240px or larger
- **Standard Format**: 120px × 120px to 240px × 240px
- **Small Format**: 64px × 64px to 120px × 120px
- **Icon Format**: 32px × 32px to 64px × 64px

#### Print Size Guidelines
- **Business Cards**: 24mm × 24mm minimum
- **Letterhead**: 20mm × 20mm minimum
- **Brochures**: 30mm × 30mm minimum
- **Signage**: 100mm × 100mm minimum

### Clear Space Requirements

#### Minimum Clear Space
- **Primary Logo**: 0.5x logo height minimum
- **Simplified Logo**: 0.25x logo height minimum
- **Icon Logo**: 2px minimum
- **Monochrome Logo**: 0.25x logo height minimum

#### Recommended Clear Space
- **All Logos**: 1x logo height recommended
- **Premium Applications**: 1.5x logo height
- **Crowded Applications**: 0.5x logo height minimum

#### Clear Space Calculation
```
Clear Space = Logo Height × Multiplier
Example: 120px logo × 0.5 = 60px clear space minimum
```

- Contrast: Good
- Professionalism: High

✅ **Medium Gray + Cultural Gold**
- Usage: Secondary emphasis

✅ **Dark Gray + White**
- Usage: High contrast text
- Contrast: Excellent
- Professionalism: High

### Color Restrictions

#### Prohibited Color Combinations
❌ **Low Contrast Combinations**
- Do not use similar colors together
- Do not use low contrast backgrounds
- Do not violate accessibility guidelines
- Do not use colors that compete with logo

❌ **Unauthorized Colors**
- Do not use non-brand colors
- Do not create custom color variations
- Do not use outdated brand colors
- Do not mix with other brand colors

❌ **Color Modifications**
- Do not change logo color relationships
- Do not apply color gradients to logo
- Do not use color filters on logo
- Do not change color opacity

### Accessibility Requirements

#### Contrast Ratios
- **Normal Text**: 4.5:1 minimum contrast ratio
- **Large Text**: 3:1 minimum contrast ratio
- **UI Elements**: 3:1 minimum contrast ratio
- **Graphics**: 3:1 minimum for adjacent colors

#### Testing Requirements
- Test all color combinations for accessibility
- Use automated accessibility tools
- Test with color blindness simulators
- Test with low vision simulators

## Typography Usage Rules

### Logo Typography

#### Typography Integration
- Logo typography is part of the logo design
- Do not separate typography from logo elements
- Do not change logo typography
- Do not add additional text to logo

#### Typography Rules
- Use Inter Modified for logo typography
- Do not use alternative fonts for logo
- Do not change typography weight or style
- Do not modify typography spacing

### Context Typography

#### Typography Pairings
✅ **Approved Pairings**
- Inter Modified + Inter
- Inter Modified + System Fonts
- Inter Modified + Monospace

❌ **Prohibited Pairings**
- Inter Modified + Comic Sans
- Inter Modified + Decorative Fonts
- Inter Modified + Outdated Fonts
- Inter Modified + Inappropriate Fonts

## Quality Control

### File Quality Standards

#### File Formats
✅ **Approved Formats**
- SVG for digital use
- PNG for raster images
- AI for source files
- EPS for print use

❌ **Prohibited Formats**
- GIF for logo use (except simple animations)
- BMP for professional use
- Low-quality JPEG files
- Uncompressed files

#### Resolution Requirements
- **Digital**: 72 PPI minimum
- **Print**: 300 PPI minimum
- **Large Format**: 150 PPI minimum
- **Mobile**: 150 PPI minimum

#### File Size Guidelines
- **SVG Files**: < 5KB
- **PNG Files**: < 50KB
- **Print Files**: < 10MB
- **Source Files**: < 50MB
